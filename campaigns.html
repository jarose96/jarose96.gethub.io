College Affordability

 We’re working to relieve some of the burden of student debt by making textbooks free. Nationally, students spend $1200 on textbooks per semester, and then only get a small fraction of that back if they take the time to resell them. Read More

Antibiotics

 We’re working to flip the food marketplace! Factory farms are raising their animals on human antibiotics to make them fatter faster. But all that medication in a cramped space means that the few animals that have mutated genes that can overcome those antibiotics and create superbugs. These superbugs are infecting humans and our antibiotics won’t work on them! So people are dying. Read More

New Voters Project

 We’re working to make North Carolina’s democracy work better for everyone, starting right here at UNC. Our democratic institutions and voter participation in NC are much farther behind the rest of the nation. Our districts have been deemed racially gerrymandered and we don’t have simple things like online voter registration, like South Carolina does. Read More

Mission Statement

NCPIRG is an advocate for the public interest. When consumers are cheated, or our natural environment is threatened, or the voices of ordinary citizens are drowned out by special interest lobbyists, NCPIRG speaks up and takes action. We uncover threats to public health and well-being and fight to end them, using the time-tested tools of investigative research, media exposés, grassroots organizing, advocacy and litigation. NCPIRG’s mission is to deliver persistent, result-oriented public interest activism that protects consumers, encourages a fair, sustainable economy, and fosters responsive, democratic government.
